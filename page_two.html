ARTIST'S STATEMENT


Jessica Smith - As a painter, I primarily work in acrylic and my style is considered
"art-deco." This art 74 class has been my first formal introduction to digital media. My work has primarily drawn
from two inspirations, one being my love and reverence for nature. I like to create direct contrast with natural
elements to emphasize how far-removed from nature we have become. Another inspiration for me is my reflections on
society and social dynamism. I am really interested in how people have developed our society into what it
currently through their own personal and individual attitudes and behaviors. Using computers and computer systems as
a medium for art has begun a paradigm shift for me and my relationship with art as well as my own personal and
professional artistic expression.
