ARTIST'S STATEMENT


I am a visual artist based in East Bay, CA. I primarily work in acrylic paint and my style is considered
"art-deco." This art 74 class has been my first formal introduction to digital media. My work has primarily drawn
from two inspirations, one being my love and reverence for nature. I like to create direct contrast with natural
elements to emphasize how far-removed from nature human beings have become. Another inspiration for me is my reflections on
society and social dynamism. I am really interested in how people have developed our society into what it is
currently through our collective and individual attitudes and behaviors. Using computers and computer systems as
a medium for art has been an extremely rewarding challenge and has had a big impact on my personal as well as
professional artistic process and expression.
